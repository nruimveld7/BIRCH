Events Popup Overview
The Events popup provides an overview of the scheduled events for a particular scope on any given day.

Double click on any cell in the main schedule view to access this menu for that scope on the selected day.

There are three scopes to which an event can be applied:
Full Day - applies to every team member that is assigned to any shift on the selected day
Shift - applies to every team member that is assigned to the selected shift on the selected day
Member - applies only to the team member that is selected on the selected day
